New Album

Coming soon

Fire Walk With Us literally arose from the ashes, when in 2015 their rehearsal studio burned down and all equipment was lost. The search for a new home and rebuilding the band postponed the release of their second full-length album. This winter it will finally see the light of day. Its five tracks encompass the expansiveness of postrock and the hard-hitting tones of metal in equal measure. The album was recorded by Quintijn Verhoef at Studio Independent Recordings, mixed by the band themselves and mastered by Robbe K. at Audiovisualz.

Breaking news! New record on the way/new bass player

It’s been a while since our last update, quite a while we might add. Late 2014 we recorded an album we were very excited about, but then disaster struck. Early 2015 our practice space burnt down and basically all of our equipment was lost. What we also lost was momentum. It took a lot of time to reinvigorate the band. The past is the past, fast forward to now. We’re excited to announce that the album will finally see the light of day somewhere in the coming months.

On a sad note, earlier this year Geert decided to leave the band. His decision was entirely personal. He was a founding member and his tour-of-bass-duty lasted a decade, but time and things change. We want to thank him for his friendship and dedication. Good luck with future endeavours!

We’re not without a bass player however, since Ronald van Wonderen and his 5-string recently joined us. Welcome, Ronald! We’re busy rehearsing all our material, including an album’s worth of new material we want to record as soon as possible. Check out our new website and stay tuned for more updates!
